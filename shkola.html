About Our School

- one of the first non-state educational institutions in the city of Khujand. Founded in 1996, the school has earned a reputation as an educational institution that combines the traditions of classical education with modern educational standards.

Our Advantages

- Qualified teachers. All school teachers have high qualifications and many years of experience. - Modern conditions. Spacious classrooms, modern teaching materials, and access to digital technologies. - Individual approach. We take into account the individual characteristics of each student, helping to reveal their potential. - Extracurricular activities. Additional classes, clubs, and events contribute to the development of talents and socialization.

Educational Programs

Primary education (grades 1-4). - Basic education (grades 5-8). - Secondary education (grades 9-11). The school curriculum is based on state educational standards with elements of in-depth study of mathematics, foreign languages, and natural sciences.

Infrastructure

- Spacious building with an area of 2980 sq. m. - Comfortable classrooms and modern equipment. - Library, computer classrooms, and sports grounds.
